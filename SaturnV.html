Saturn V

Saturn V is the only rocket capable of taking men to the moon. It was used during the Apollo missions and first landed on the moon in 1969. The Soviet Union made a moon rocket too during what is now called the "space race" which was a race to land people on the moon. Unfortunately the Soviet rocket (N1) blew up during the test stages and never flew again
