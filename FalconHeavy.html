Falcon Heavy

Falcon Heavy is a partially reusable three-stage-to-orbit heavy-lift launch vehicle designed and manufactured by SpaceX. It is powered by 27 Merlin engines, also developed by SpaceX, burning cryogenic liquid oxygen and rocket-grade kerosene (RP-1) as propellants The falcon heavy is made up of one strengthened falcon 9 with two strap-on first stages.
